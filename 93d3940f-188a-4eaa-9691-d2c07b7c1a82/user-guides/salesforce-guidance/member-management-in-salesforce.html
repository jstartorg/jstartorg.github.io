During this stage in the process, the highlights panel on the Volunteer Registration will show the member's criminal history check Status alongside a list of CHC requirements. You can also enter key details here like Verified Exemption date and Service Start Date.

Criminal History Checks

  • If a certain check is not required or is covered by another check, it will say N/A, and you won't need to enter any data there.

  • If the check is required but has not yet been initiated, there will be space for you to enter the Initiation Date.

  • After the Initiation Date is entered, there will be space for you to enter the Clearance Date.

  • Once all required criminal history checks have been completed and their date fields have been filled out, this will automatically move the criminal history check Status to Cleared for Training and Service and Registration Stage to Serving.

Helpful Tools

In the actions panel, you can use the following buttons to facilitate certain steps.

Invite to Video Chat

  • This emails the member a link to your personal video chat room on 8x8.vc.
  • To access the chat room yourself, go to https://8x8.vc/jstart/yourfirstname.yourlastname (replace yourfirstname.yourlastname with your name as it appears in your Jumpstart email)

Run NSOPW

  • This opens the NSOPW website in a new tab and searches member's name
  • Print results (you can also save the results as a pdf to the Volunteer Registration's Secure Documents)

Enroll in My AmeriCorps

  • This button will display on records for AmeriCorps members. It opens the eGrants website in a new tab and prefills the enrollment form with details from the Volunteer Registration.

Quick Copy Details

  • For external forms that we cannot prefill, you can quickly copy key details from the record such as name, phone number, birthday, and social security number without worrying if you missed a character.

On your home page, you can enter Initiation and Clearance Dates in bulk by going to the Criminal History Checks tab of the current member and child information panel.

Serving

During this stage in the process, you can assign the member to a Member Team, give them permission to enter data into JStats, enter their Service Start and End Dates, and manage stipend information.

Member Teams

To assign a member to a Member Team, click the edit icon next to Member Team and search and select an existing record, or click + New Member Team to create a new one.

  • Give the Member Team a Name
  • Be sure the correct Program Year is selected
  • Assign the team to a Classroom
  • Manage your team schedule in the Schedule section
  • Manage team planning in the Team Planning Section

Exiting

You can complete electronic versions of member exit forms from directly within the Volunteer Registration record.

Performance Review

  • Expand the Performance Review accordion tab in the highlights panel

  • Fill out the form and click Save Performance Review at the bottom to save your progress.

    • When you are finished filling out the form, sign the Supervisor Signature part. You must sign in one motion, because once there is any content in the signature field, the rest of the form will lock for editing so that the member cannot edit your answers. If you mess up your signature, do not click Save Performance Review, just refresh the page to sign again. Once you sign and click Save Performance Review, this will send the member an email with a link to sign their own portion of the form.
      • If the member does not receive the email, you can click the Resend Performance Review button in the actions panel to send it again.
    • The second page of the performance review will not appear until at least 10 minutes after the member signs their part. This is to prevent members from signing the second page themselves. After the 10-minute delay, you can refresh the page and fill out and sign the second page.
      • If the member never signs the form or responds to your attempts to contact, you can move the FOR STAFF: An Attempt to Contact process has been followed and this member can/will not sign this form. toggle to "Show" to display the second page.
    • Once you sign and save the second page, this will automatically save a pdf of the form to the Volunteer Registration's Secure Documents. You can view and print the completed form from there.

  • Exit Form

    • Expand the Exit Form accordion tab in the highlights panel
    • Fill out the form and click Save Exit Form at the bottom to save your progress.
    • When you are finished filling out the form, sign the Supervisor Signature part. You must sign in one motion, because once there is any content in the signature field, the rest of the form will lock for editing so that the member cannot edit your answers. If you mess up your signature, do not click Save Exit Form, just refresh the page to sign again. Once you sign and click Save Exit Form, this will send the member an email with a link to sign their own portion of the form.
      • If the member does not receive the email, you can click the Resend Exit Form button in the actions panel to send it again.
    • Once the member signs their part, this will automatically save a pdf of the form to the Volunteer Registration's Secure Documents. You can view and print the completed form from there.

Once you complete these forms and all other components of the exit process, enter the Service Exit Date. If the Service Exit Date is before or equal to the current date when the record is modified, this will automatically move the Registration Stage to Exited.
