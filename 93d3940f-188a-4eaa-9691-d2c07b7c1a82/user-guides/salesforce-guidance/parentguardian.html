Parent/Guardian Form Guidelines

In general, forms where an applicantâ€™s minor status is relevant will automatically fill in the relevant details. A best practice is to try to get a Parent/Guardian (PG) email address before sending any forms, though in most cases the process will fall back to the Corps Member's (CM) own email, with space for them to add the PG email when they sign. Exceptions are starred (*) below.

Authorization Form

The Jumpstart authorization form will be sent to the CM first, allowing them to review and sign. If they are a minor they will be required to enter a PG email (if one is not on their record), as well as the PG name. When they submit the form, it will send an email to the PG asking them to sign the form as well. After they have signed, the form will be saved to their record in Salesforce.

Parent/Guardian Form

This form will send to the PG email address if present, or the Corps Member (CM) if not. Since the PG form only needs to be signed by the PG themselves, there are no extra process steps. The CM can forward the email they receive to their PG if necessary and the link within it will still work for them.

Jumpstart Enrollment Package

The standard enrollment package will determine if the CM is a minor and require them to fill in the PG email on the form if it is not already provided in their record. The CM signature and submission will then trigger an email to the PG for them to review and sign as well. Once the PG has completed their portion of the contract, the final email to the CM's supervisor will be sent allowing completion of the enrollment package.

AmeriCorps Member Contracts for University of Missouri Sites*

Both UM sites have a custom contract which requires the Parent/Guardian Email field to be filled in before the button to send will appear, as their signature process will not allow for email addresses to be added later.
