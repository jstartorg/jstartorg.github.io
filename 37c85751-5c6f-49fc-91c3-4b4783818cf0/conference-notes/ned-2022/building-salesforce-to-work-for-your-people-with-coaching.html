Coach Salesforce Leaders

  • Building a relationship over time to reach coachee’s goals
  • Org should have these SF roles
    • Engineer
      • User liaison & educator
      • Systems builder
      • UX storyteller
    • Strategist
      • Executive liaison & educator
      • Systems visionary
      • ROI storyteller
  • Align with stakeholders on SF goals and user support needs
  • Promote or hire SF team members to drive progress
  • Set outcome and learning goals for SF team members
  • Connect SF team members with a coach to meet regularly (weekly)
    • Set clear project goals
    • Share knowledge and then assign homework
    • Check in on roadblocks next time
    • Coaching vs mentoring
      • Coach: Equal who helps people see their strengths and skills and cheers them on and holds them accountable along the way
      • Mentor: has more knowledge, more of a guide
  • Share the story of progress and roadblocks with stakeholders

Co-create with users

  • Involve users from the beginning in the inception of the idea of how to use SF mor effectively, get feedback and iterate together
  • Hold a SF users & leaders listening tour
    • What value is Sf bringing to you?
    • What do you think we use SF for?
    • What are the pain points?
    • Are roles and expectations clear?
    • What are your biggest SF dreams?
  • Share baseline & learnings of the listening tour
  • Name the first North Star & MVP you’re heading to
  • Create an agile feedback & improvement plan (building and releasing work through stages)
    • Presenter's method is quarterly objectives with two-week sprints
    • Share out to everyone in the org at the end of the sprint
  • Share the story of the why, the journey, and what’s next

Support your team to success

  • Create a transparent support request management system (put them on home page)
  • Assess patterns of user needs and learning preferences
  • Create a role-based knowledge hub and hold learning Q&As and 1:1s
  • Enable users to lean in as Salesforce champions
  • Communicate what is needed for success to stakeholders
