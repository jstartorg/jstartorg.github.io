Serving

During this stage in the process, you can assign the member to a Member Team and manage stipend information as applicable.

Member Teams

To assign a member to a Member Team, click the edit icon next to Member Team and search and select an existing record, or click + New Member Team to create a new one.

  • Give the Member Team a Name
  • Be sure the correct Program Year is selected
  • Assign the team to a Classroom
  • Manage your team schedule in the Schedule section
  • Manage team planning in the Team Planning Section

Stipends

If the member is receiving a stipend:

  • Ensure that the Benefits Type is Stipend.
  • Fill in the Stipend Funding Source in order to use electronic stipend forms (see Helpful Tools below).
  • Follow additional instructions provided by Finance on Corps Member stipends in Salesforce and Paycom.
  • The Stipend Management tab on your home page will show you a list of members in the Serving stage with Stipend selected as the Benefits Type, with the stipend paperwork they have submitted checked off. Click on the list name to expand it.

Helpful Tools

In the actions panel, you can use the following buttons to facilitate certain steps during this stage. See here for more about the information needed to make these buttons available, and to move the record to the next stage.

  • Send Stipend Agreement: Emails the member a prefilled Stipend Agreement form for them to sign. Once they sign, it will save as a Secure Document.
  • Send Position Change Form: Emails the member a prefilled Position Change form for them to sign. If they are a minor, their parent/guardian will also have to sign their part. Once they submit, you will receive an email prompting you to sign your part. It will then save as a Secure Document.
  • Sign Mid-Term Performance Review: Opens a prefilled Mid-Term Performance Review form for you to sign. Once you submit, it will email the member prompting them to sign their part. It will then save as a Secure Document.
  • Sign Rejecting Previously Approved Timesheets Action Plan: Opens a prefilled Rejecting Previously Approved Timesheets Action Plan form for you to sign. Once you submit, it will save as a Secure Document.
  • Sign and Send Performance Improvement Plan: Opens a prefilled Performance Improvement Plan form for you to sign. Once you submit, it will email the member prompting them to sign their part. It will then save as a Secure Document.
