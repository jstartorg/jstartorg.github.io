Description

Office 365 Groups, unlike Active Directory groups, are not just a collection of members. They are a full environment that by default include a true group, a SharePoint site, a Teams team, and other elements. They are "heavy" and do not sync automatically with other group memberships on-prem. The also generate an email address based on their display name at creation, so can easily take up important addresses if one is not careful. For instance an early adopter of Teams at one site made themselves a group just called "Corps Members", which meant that the email address corpsmembers@jstart.org now belong to that site and person exclusively, which is not a good idea.

Uses

O365 Groups are not only communications platforms, but can be used to give permissions in other SharePoint sites, share data sets in Power BI, designate automatic license assignment, and automatic App Registration assignment which authorizes SSO access in several systems that are not meant for everyone.
